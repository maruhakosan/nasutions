The Quintuplets

◆
Varen
Varen Togar Nasution ① The Exhausted Anchor

Carries everything. Asks for nothing. The natural leader who ensures everyone else is okay first — and quietly deteriorates second. His anger is terrifyingly calm; he whispers instead of yelling, which is considerably worse than yelling.

Guntur
Guntur Sanggam Nasution ② The Grumpy Giant

Permanent scowl. Faint eyebrow scar. Physically cannot let people be uncomfortable. Will shove food at you without warning or explanation and then pretend it never happened. Loves deeply in the language of brute force and rendang.

Kaivan
Kaivan Pandu Nasution ③ The Artsy Playboy

Silver rings. Messy hair. Studies your micro-expressions before you finish your sentence. Needs freedom — until suddenly he doesn't. Leaves people on read for hours only to send a blurry indie photo to show he cares.

Mahesa
Mahesa Saut Nasution ④ The Loud One

Always a song in his head, a guitar pick in his pocket. Breaks tension by being too much, on purpose, until it works. Requires significant external validation and will pay you back with unreasonable amounts of warmth.

Anggara
Anggara Sahat Nasution ⑤ The Silent Genius

Recognizes people by their footstep cadence. Fixes your problems before you notice them. Sudden physical touch causes internal system crash. Strictly communicates via Google Maps pins, calendar invites, and "Done."
